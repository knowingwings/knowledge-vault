## Calisthenics Session (~30-45 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Weighted jump rope: 2-3 min
- Dynamic stretches

**Main Circuit (20-30 min)** - 3 rounds
- Push ups (push up bars): 10-15 reps
- Ab roller: 8-12 reps
- Resistance band rows: 12-15 reps
- Resistance band squats: 15 reps
- Plank hold: 30-60 sec
- Rest: 60 sec between rounds

**Finisher (5-10 min)**
- Weighted jump rope: 3-5 min intervals
- Core work: leg raises, bicycle crunches

**Cool-down (5 min)**
- Static stretches on yoga mat

---

## Plyometrics Session - Tuesday (~30-40 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Light jump rope
- Dynamic stretches
- Leg swings

**Main Work (20-25 min)**
- Box jumps (or tuck jumps): 3x8
- Burpees: 3x10
- Jump squats: 3x12
- Lateral bounds: 3x10 each side
- Jump lunges: 3x8 each leg
- Rest: 90 sec between sets

**Cool-down (5-10 min)**
- Light stretching
- Foam rolling if available

## Weekly Review (Sunday 20:00-22:00, 90-120 min)

**Part 1: Blank Page Recall (15 min)**
- What were the key concepts from this week?
- Write from memory first, no notes

**Part 2: Compare & Identify Gaps (15 min)**
- Open notes, compare to recall
- Note what you missed
- Identify weak areas

**Part 3: Process Reflection (15 min)**
- What study/work method worked well?
- What felt ineffective?
- What adjustment will I try next week?

**Part 4: Connection & Exploration (15 min)**
- What unexpected link did I notice across domains?
- What would I like to explore further?
- Update permanent notes with new connections

**Part 5: Verbal Practice (15 min)**
- Pick one concept from this week
- Explain at 3 levels: expert, layperson, ELI5

**Part 6: Planning (15-30 min)**
- Review quarterly priorities - on track?
- Review past week: What got done? What didn't?
- Identify next week's 3 most important outcomes
- Time-block priorities for the week
- Identify potential obstacles

**Part 7: Git & Admin (5-10 min)**
- `vault-save` - commit week's notes
- Review essay seeds
- Quick vault maintenance

## Run Day Nutrition Quick Reference

**Pre-Run (long runs 60+ min only)**
- Small snack 30-60 min before (banana, toast)
- Short runs: can run fasted

**Post-Run (within 30-60 min)**
- Prioritise protein + carbs
- Examples: Eggs on toast, porridge with protein, yoghurt with fruit

**Long Run Days (Thu/Sun)**
- Increase carbs day before and day of
- Extra snack in afternoon if needed
- Hydration: Extra 500ml-1L water

**Daily Hydration:** 2-3L water (extra 500ml-1L on run days)
