# Plate-Harmonic Reducer

Hub for the reducer R&D thread: design, prototyping, the bench test stand, and the rover-arm joints it feeds.

**The work.** Exploratory plate-harmonic reducer (a profiled-groove design from the literature), chosen for novelty and 3D-printability for fast, cheap iteration. Feeds the Team Bath Roving arm joints. See [[rover-arm-component-order]].

**Status (22/06/2026).** First prototype failed. Open question: teeth not engaging, or not enough clearance to disengage and let the flex spline rotate. Faster iteration now via the uni lab.

**Test stand.** Dedicated bench rig for empirical characterisation (gear ratio, backlash, repeatability, speed sweep, endurance).
