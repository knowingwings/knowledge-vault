# Review & Planning Rituals

## Overview

Regular reviews ensure you stay aligned with goals, catch problems early, and continuously improve. These rituals integrate with the Polymath Operating System.

---

## Daily Reflection (5 min) - Before Bed ~22:00

### Questions
1. What did I learn today that I didn't know yesterday?
2. What confused me? (Valuable information, not failure)
3. What connection did I notice?

### Quick Capture
- Any essay seeds?
- Questions for 48-hour retrieval?
- Tomorrow's top priority?

### Location
Add to daily note in `6-daily/` or quick capture in `0-inbox/`

---

## Weekly Review (90-120 min) - Sunday Evening

### Timing
- After weekly clean (19:00-20:00)
- Review session: 20:00-22:00

### Structure

**Part 1: Blank Page Recall (15 min)**
- What were the key concepts from this week?
- Write from memory first, no notes

**Part 2: Compare & Identify Gaps (15 min)**
- Open notes, compare to recall
- Note what you missed
- Identify weak areas

**Part 3: Process Reflection (15 min)**
- What study/work method worked well?
- What felt ineffective?
- What adjustment will I try next week?

**Part 4: Connection & Exploration (15 min)**
- What unexpected link did I notice across domains?
- What would I like to explore further?
- Update permanent notes with new connections

**Part 5: Verbal Practice (15 min)**
- Pick one concept from this week
- Explain at 3 levels: expert, layperson, ELI5

**Part 6: Planning (15-30 min)**
- Review quarterly priorities - on track?
- Review past week: What got done? What didn't?
- Identify next week's 3 most important outcomes
- Time-block priorities for the week
- Identify potential obstacles

**Part 7: Git & Admin (5-10 min)**
- `vault-save` - commit week's notes
- Review essay seeds
- Quick vault maintenance

## Monthly Review (60 min) - Last Sunday of Month

### Timing
- Replace weekly review with extended monthly review
- Or do monthly on Saturday, weekly on Sunday

### Structure

**Knowledge Audit (15 min)**
- What topics covered this month?
- Where are the gaps?
- What to prioritise next month?

**Articulation Check (10 min)**
- Pick 3 concepts from this month
- Can you ELI5 them?
- Where do you struggle to explain clearly?

**Progress on Quarterly Priorities (15 min)**
- Priority 1: % complete, blockers
- Priority 2: % complete, blockers
- Priority 3: % complete, blockers

**Nutrition & Fitness Review (10 min)**
- Physical impact: energy, recovery, performance
- Financial: weekly average, total spend, vs budget
- Adjustments needed?

**Planning Adjustments (10 min)**
- Habit consistency check
- What connections between learning and goals emerged?
- Adjustments for next month

## Quarterly Review (Half Day) - End of Quarter

### Timing
- Last weekend of quarter
- 3-4 hours focused time

### Structure

1. **Complete Quarterly Sprint Review**
   - Each priority: Complete / Partial / Missed
   - What worked, what didn't

2. **Assess Progress on Annual Objectives**
   - Each objective: on track / behind / ahead

3. **Review 5-Year Goals**
   - Still relevant?
   - Need adjustment?

4. **Plan Next Quarter's Priorities**
   - Max 3 priorities
   - Definition of done for each
   - Weekly milestones

5. **Identify Skill Gaps**
   - What's holding you back?
   - What to learn next quarter?

6. **Financial Review**
   - Quarter spend vs budget
   - Investment performance
   - Adjustments needed

7. **Celebrate Wins**
   - Acknowledge progress
   - Note in wins document

### Q1 Review Date
- **Saturday, March 28** or **Sunday, March 29**

---

## Annual Review (Full Day) - Late December

### Timing
- Between Christmas and New Year
- Full day dedicated

### Structure

1. Complete annual plan review
2. Life domains audit - how's each area?
3. Review 10-year vision - update if needed
4. Update 5-year goals
5. Create next year's annual plan
6. Review git history - how has thinking evolved?
7. Gratitude practice
