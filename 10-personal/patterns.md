# Self-Knowledge Patterns

Hub for the recurring personal patterns that surface across the dailies and reviews: the validated mechanisms behind how I actually work.

- **Pre-commitment / run-treatment:** self-set deadlines do not bind; pre-decided or external ones do.
- **Burnout baseline:** recent productivity is partly LLM-assisted and may be fragile. See [[burnout-motivation-energy]].
- **Hydration** is a foundational layer; **fitness links to motivation**.
- **Productive procrastination:** bank valuable low-friction work when avoiding a high-friction task.
- **Silent-plans tax**; **environment as substrate**.

On a rough day, external evidence outranks the low-state narrative. See [[wins]].
